## Product Range

### Categories

1. **LED Light Therapy Devices** — Face mask ($399), Neck system ($299)
2. **Micro-Infusion Systems** — Serum-infused micro-dart patches (bundles $109-$539)
3. **Topical Serums** — All-In-One Face Serum ($79)
4. **Water Filters** — Faucet ($129), Shower ($129)
5. **Hair Growth** — LLLT Helmet ($999)
6. **Accessories** — Silk pillowcase, Dermal Mist ($29)
7. **Gift Cards**
8. **Bundles** — Multi-product bundles with significant discounts (up to 50% off)

### Price Range

$29 (Dermal Mist) to $999 (Hair Growth Helmet)
**Sweet spot:** $109-$399

### Problem-Based Collections

- Fine Lines & Wrinkles
- Dark Spots
- Acne / Breakout Control
- Pores & Texture

### Key Observation for Skingenetix

QU:RE sells **devices + consumables** (razors-and-blades model). Their bundles combine devices with refill patches/serums. Skingenetix's product strategy should define whether it's devices, topicals, or both.

### Closest Standard Shopify Themes

Since QU:RE uses a fully custom theme, the closest standard alternatives for Skingenetix would be:

| Theme                       | Price | Why It's Similar                                                                   | Gap vs QU:RE                                          |
| --------------------------- | ----- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| **Impact** (by Maestrooo)   | $380  | Dark mode support, bold imagery, DTC-focused, conversion tools, editorial sections | Missing custom quiz, micro-infusion-specific sections |
| **Prestige** (by Maestrooo) | $400  | Luxury feel, before/after slider, shop-the-look, premium positioning               | Less conversion-focused than Impact                   |

| **Refresh** (free)          | Free  | Storytelling, ingredient focus, certifications                                     | No dark mode, basic compared to premium               |

### Recommendation for Skingenetix

**Impact** ($380) is the closest match to QU:RE's look and feel — dark mode, bold imagery,
conversion-focused features (quick buy, sticky cart, cross-selling, promo banners).
If budget allows, this is the theme to get.
If not, Sense (free) is the best free alternative,
but will need more custom CSS work to achieve the premium dark look.

## Impact on Theme Decision

Based on this competitor analysis, the theme recommendation changes from the initial research:

### Previous Recommendation: Sense or Refresh (free)

### Updated Recommendation: **Impact** ($380) or **Prestige** ($400)

**Reason:** QU:RE uses a custom dark theme with premium DTC aesthetics. A free theme (Sense/Refresh) would look noticeably less premium in comparison. The **Impact** theme ($380) provides:

- Dark mode support
- Bold imagery and editorial sections
- Conversion features (sticky cart, quick buy, cross-selling, promo banners)
- Modern DTC brand feel matching QU:RE's aesthetic
- Standard Shopify 2.0 architecture (Claude-friendly)

**Trade-off:** $380 one-time cost vs looking like a premium competitor from day one.
